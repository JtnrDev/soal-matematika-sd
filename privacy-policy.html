Advertisements

To keep the App free for all users, it displays advertisements. We use the Google AdMob SDK (Software Development Kit) as our advertising provider.

Since this App is directed to children, the AdMob SDK is configured to comply with Google Play's Families Policy and applicable children's privacy regulations. This means that personalized advertising is disabled for all users of the App.

Even with personalized ads disabled, the AdMob SDK may collect certain non-personal identifiers, such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., Android Advertising ID or Device ID)
  • IP address
  • Usage information

This data is used by the AdMob SDK solely to serve contextual ads (ads based on the content of the app), limit the number of times the same ad is shown (frequency capping), and prevent fraudulent activity.

We select ad providers that are safe for kids. However, while we take steps to filter inappropriate content, we do not have complete control over every single advertisement that appears, as the Ad Network ultimately determines the specific ad content from their inventory. Any data collected by the AdMob SDK is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
